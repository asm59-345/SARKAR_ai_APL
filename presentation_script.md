# 🎬 SarkarAI OS — Master Video Presentation Script & Screenplay

This document serves as the official script, storyboard, and screenplay blueprint for your hackathon video presentation. Use it as a guide to record a highly polished, engaging, and professional **3 to 4 minute demonstration** of **SarkarAI OS** for your Agentic Premier League (APL) submission.

---

## 📽️ Production & Recording Guidelines
* **Recording Tool**: Use OBS Studio, Loom, or Camtasia to capture high-definition (1080p, 60fps) screen recordings.
* **Vocal Tone**: Maintain a clear, energetic, confident, and professional tone.
* **Visual Mode**: Ensure your browser is set to **Dark Mode** to showcase the glowing glassmorphic and neon cyber styles.
* **Transition Pacing**: Spend 10–15 seconds showing each key feature, allowing the viewer to absorb the UI details.

## 🎙️ Production Tips for Team Asar_ai
1. **Do a Practice Run**: Speak slowly. Hindi/Awadhi cultural terms should be pronounced clearly to make the presentation feel authentic to Lucknow.
2. **Cursor Highlighting**: Enable cursor click highlights in your recording software (e.g. OBS or Loom) so judges can follow your mouse navigation easily across the dark glassmorphic cards.
3. **Sound Check**: Record in a quiet room with a standard microphone, using slight background noise suppression if possible.
